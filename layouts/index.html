Q&A

What do you do?

I'm a designer and developer focused on building immersive 3D experiences for the web. My work spans interactive installations, product visualization, and experimental interfaces.

How did you get into 3D?

It started with a curiosity about how physical objects could be represented digitally. I picked up Blender during a long winter break and never really stopped. The pipeline from sculpt to real-time render still feels like magic.
